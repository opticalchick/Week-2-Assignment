There are so many wonderful areas to explore in Mexico City. CDMX (as Mexico City is known) is home to over 170 museums to visit. Some of these include:

  • Palacio de Bellas Artes


  • Castillo de Chapultepec /Museo Nacional de Historia


  • The Frida Kahlo Museum


Aside from Museums, there are many other wonderful places to explore, especially if you love to be outside. Two or our favorites were the Unesco World Heritage site of Xochimildo, and the other was Chapultepec Park. There are also an abundance of parks, bursting with beautiful flowers, trees, and tremendous art sculptures.
